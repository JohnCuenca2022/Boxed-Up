DELIVERY

We have our own delivery service within Quezon City, Caloocan City, Manila and Mandaluyong City and we also do Third-party delivery shouldered by the customer.


Delivery service and fee will be discussed through Facebook Messenger after sending the order code and screenshot of the payment.


As of now we only cater orders coming from metro manila (preferably from Quezon City, Caloocan City, Manila and Mandaluyong City) to ensure the quality of the food.


Accepting orders is at 8am – 5pm, and our delivery service starts at 10am.


ORDERS

To ensure a smooth transaction, we use Gcash as the primary payment method. The cash on delivery option is only available when using our own delivery service.


After placing an order, expect a pop-up on the website within 10 minutes if the order is accepted or rejected. The pop-up contains the order code, QR code of GCash and the link to Facebook page of Boxed Up.


For the 3rd Party delivery, we will text or send you a message of their tracking details. For the Delivery Service of our own, we will send you an update through messenger or text.


We do next day delivery to avoid the food being delivered cold. Time will be decided by the customer however, please be advised that we will notify you of any delays where there is a situation where we can’t control such as weather, traffic and other factors.


There are a few factors that can make us decline your order, first is the availability of ingredients and the next one is your location. To guarantee the quality and freshness of the food we do not cater orders outside of metro manila and our service area.


There are two ways to cancel your order

1. Do not continue the ordering process by NOT messaging the order verification code to Boxed Up's Facebook.

2. Message Boxed Up's Facebook and manually request for your order to be cancelled. However, Orders cannot be canceled once the order fulfilment process has started.


As of the moment we do not have a physical store. But we have one on the works! Stay tuned for more updates!


If you already have an account, you can order by doing the following:

1. Go to the menu page and select your desired food.

2. Proceed to checkout page, and press “Place order”.

3. Within 10 minutes, you will receive a notification on the website once your order is accepted. Order code will be indicated along with the QR code of the GCash for payment and the link to the Boxed Up Facebook for you to message the order code and wait for the reply for your total amount with delivery fee.


Yes, you can order after store hours however, your order will be processed the next day. For Example, if you ordered 6pm on a Monday, we will process your order next day (Tuesday) and deliver your order the very next day (Wednesday) delivery hours.


ACCOUNTS

A. Using Facebook or Google

1. Go to the login page

2. Click either Facebook or Google login

3. Select your account and enter your credentials

4. Press ok, then you are good to go


B. Register using your email

1. Go to the login page

2. Click on the sign up link

3. Enter a valid email address

4. Check your email for a verification message

5. Click the link in the verification message

6. Enter a strong password and Sign up

7. You will be redirected to your profile page. Enter your info then you are good to go
